FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What hardware do I need?
We support all major VR headsets including Meta Quest, HTC Vive, and Valve Index. A VR-ready PC may be required for certain premium experiences.
Can I try it before buying?
Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial on our Basic and Pro plans, giving you limited access to explore our library.
Does VR cause motion sickness?
Our zero-latency edge servers and optimized software drastically reduce the chance of motion sickness for most users.
Are there age restrictions?
Our platform is generally recommended for users 13 and older, in line with most VR headset manufacturer guidelines.
